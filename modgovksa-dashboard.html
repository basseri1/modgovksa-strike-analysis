Key Observations

14x Escalation in 2 Weeks

Attack tempo surged from ~5 drones/day (Mar 6) to 70 drones/day (Mar 20), showing a dramatic and sustained escalation.

Oil Infrastructure Focus (Week 1)

Shaybah oilfield was the most targeted asset during Mar 6-12, with multiple daily waves of drones through the Empty Quarter.

Shift to Urban Targets (Week 2+)

From mid-March, Eastern Province and Riyadh became primary targets, replacing oil infrastructure as the main focus.

Diplomatic Quarter Attacks

At least 2 separate drone attacks on the embassy district in Riyadh — a significant escalation targeting foreign diplomatic missions.

Prince Sultan Air Base Targeted

Ballistic missiles specifically aimed at the base hosting US military — showing intent to strike American military assets on Saudi soil.

Purely Factual Tone

@modgovksa never attributes attacks to Iran or includes political commentary. Posts follow a strict format: "Interception and destruction of [X] in [location]."

No Pre-March 6 Posts

Despite the war starting Feb 28, the first interception post appeared March 6 — suggesting either a delay in attacks or a delayed announcement strategy.

No Civilian Casualties Reported

The account never mentions civilian casualties. Only a few Diplomatic Quarter posts note "no material damage or civilian injuries."
